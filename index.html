About Ghumo

Ghumo is a modern ride-booking and parcel delivery platform connecting customers with verified drivers. We provide short rides, long-distance trips, and fast parcel delivery with real-time tracking and secure payments. The mobile application is currently in Google Play Closed Testing Phase.

Our Services

Book rides instantly with real-time tracking and transparent pricing.

Fast and reliable parcel delivery using our verified driver network.

📍 Live Tracking

Track rides and deliveries in real time with accurate location updates.

Empowering Drivers

Ghumo ensures transparent driver earnings with commission-based revenue sharing. Drivers can track daily income, incentives, and ride history directly in the app.

Business Model

Ghumo operates on a commission-based model where a small percentage is charged per completed ride or delivery. We focus on affordability for customers and fair earnings for drivers.
